FAQ

1. How long does a logo design take?

Ans. Standard logo customization takes upto 1 to 2 weeks. But it can go upto upto 4 weeks depending upon a customer's needs and customization.

2. What if I want to make a change to a logo?

Ans. You can either reach us through mailing the design team with the changes or from our webpage.

3. How does copyright and trademark registration work?

Ans. On completion of the logo design project, and once payment has been made, the copyright for the final agreed logo is automatically transferred to you. If needed I can also provide a copyright transfer document to give comfort that the copyright has been transferred.

4. What does it cost to hire you?

Ans. Since we put the customers’ needs ahead, we charge accordingly based on designs and how long it takes, but while buying a pre made logo with basic changes the prizes will be fixed.

5. How many different logos do you propose?

Ans. Based on the designs and color combinations we can provide the customers with a maximum of 4-8 designs n the fast draft and from there on it depends on the needs of the customers.

6. What does it require to order a logo design?

Ans. Firstly, you have to show the proper paperwork for the company which you want the logo to be designed for, second need to have an basic idea or design of the logo you want and third some advance has to be given so that we can start working on you logo.

7. Do I have to pay up any other hidden charges for my logo design?

Ans. There are no hidden charges but first you must pay an advance amount for us to start working on your first draft just as a reassurance. And after the first draft is ok, we require for you to pay 50% of the final amount and rest amount can be paid in instalments till the deisigning is completed.

8. Will I be updated on the logo design process?

Ans. As already mentioned, that the customer is given the highest priority you will be shared a google drive link with all the designs uploaded in it and you will be informed with every change we make via emails, messages on WhatsApp and calls.

9. Can I get my old logo design modified?

Ans. Yes, old logos or the logo which you previously used can be modified every bit of the way. You can totally change the design or just change the color we are here to do what you want. But we will require a proof of the logo you want us to edit as yours own.

10. What sort of support can I expect from TINEID once the logo design process is complete?

Ans. We will stay with you every step of the way till the logo is officially in use.
