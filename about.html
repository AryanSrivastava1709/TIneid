ABOUT US

Tinied designs logo uniquely and sustainably for companies. We have a history of 18K+ logos designed and delivered successfully. Tineid is also into modification and renewal of logos. Our teams uses design and technology to create high-quality branding and marketing tools so anyone from individuals to large companies can create amazing visual assets for their brand regardless of technical or artistic skills. We take pride in turning complex design and layout problems into ridiculously easy to use tools for our users. We make it possible for customers around the world to express themselves through designs, and to choose a more sustainable buisness. We create value for our customers by delivering our customer offering and by developing with a focus on sustainable and profitable growth.


The rapid shift in designs continues.
We accelerate our digital development through launching more flexible ways of digital work culture. Improved ways of working will make us more flexible, fast and efficient. Ever-increasing customer expectations mean we work passionately to offer the best combination of design, quality, price and sustainability.


Our workforce never loses sight of the customer.
We work to create value beyond profit, putting our customers at the centre of our decision-making, and always striving to do and be better. It is fundamental to how we do business that our DESIGN IS THE RIGHT CHOICE OF REPRESENTING YOU/YOUR BUISENESS.


We believe anyone can make their business, side hustle, or passion project look beautiful — even if they're not a designer. As an established design company, we make our design processes without taking away the creativity, vision, and fun that goes into building a brand identity.
